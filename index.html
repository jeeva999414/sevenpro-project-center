Your tech project partner

7 Pro supports students with end-to-end project help – from idea, to circuit & coding, to report and final explanation.

About 7 Pro How we support students
Project center focused on helping students complete quality projects with confidence.

Who we are

7 Pro (Seven Pro) is a student project lab. We support school students, diploma, engineering, arts & science students with idea to final project.

You can bring your own topic, staff-given problem statement, or select from our project ideas list. We handle: design, coding, hardware, testing, documentation and explanation.

Project process

  • 1
    Share idea & details
    Fill the online order form or send your topic on WhatsApp.
  • 2
    Call & confirmation
    Our team calls in few hours, confirms requirements, deadline & budget.
  • 3
    Development & testing
    We build the circuit / code, test everything and prepare demo.
  • 4
    Delivery & explanation
    We explain the project step by step so you can present it clearly.
